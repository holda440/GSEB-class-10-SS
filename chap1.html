ğŸ”¸ The Tribes of India

Feature: (1) Negritos (Habsi) ğŸ§‘ğŸ¿â€ğŸ¦±
Description: Considered the most ancient inhabitants. Came from Africa via Baluchistan. Physical features: black skin, 4-5 feet height, curly hair.
Feature: (2) Australoids (Nishad) ğŸ¹
Description: Came from South-East Asia. Called 'Nishad' by Aryans. Features: black skin, broad head, flat nose. Contributed to making clay utensils and cotton cloth. Tribes like Kol, Munda, and Khasi have similarities.
Feature: (3) The Dravidians ğŸŒ³
Description: Creators of Mohenjo-Daro culture (Indus Valley). Gave concepts of Mother as 'Goddess' (Parvati) and Father as 'God' (Shiva). Worshipped nature and used 'Dhup', 'Deep', 'Aarti'. Had a matriarchal family system. Shifted to South India due to Aryan dominance; their languages (Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam) are spoken there.
Feature: (4) Other Tribes (Mongoloid, Alpine, Dinarik, Armenoid) ğŸŒ
Description:
  • Mongoloids: Came from North China via Tibet. Settled in North Assam, Sikkim, Bhutan. Features: yellow complexion, flat face, chubby cheeks. Known as 'Kirat'.
  • Alpine, Dinarik, Armenoid: Migrated from Central Asia. Their genes are found in Gujarat, Saurashtra, Maharashtra, Bengal, and Odisha.
Feature: (5) The Aryans ğŸ•‰ï¸
Description: Nordic people, creators of Aryan civilization. Also called Hindus. The region they populated was 'Aryavrata' (or 'Sapta Sindhu'). They were nature lovers, worshipping trees, rivers, mountains, and composed hymns (Richas). Their amalgamation with other tribes created the composite Indian culture.
